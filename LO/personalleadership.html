PROJECT
PORTFOLIO

For my portfolio, I asked teachers for feedback, using Feedpulse or directly putting it into Figma if I missed using Feedpulse. This helped me iterate quickly and keep all the feedback handy. Working on my portfolio was fun, and I picked up some new skills along the way. Now, I'm good with Git for version control and I've got grid and flexbox down for layouts.

I also figured out that using GitLab is better than GitHub. GitLab's backed up by the school, so even if I delete stuff, my old portfolios and projects stay safe. Thanks to Maikel, I know how to smoothly move my GitHub stuff over to GitLab.

PROJECT
BRANDING

With branding I learned how to make a content strategy. I also learned what a brand guide is and how to make itterations.

Regarding the content strategy, I gained really useful insights into creating strategies that match goals and connect with the audience. By putting emphasis on what users want, I learned how to create organized plans that boost engagement and create meaningful interactions. Unfortunately, we didn't have much time left for the content strategy, which I regret. I wish we had more time to work on it so I could have crafted a stronger strategy.
For the brand guide Even though I didn't make a brand guide myself, I picked up a lot from studying them and seeing what my classmates did. Learning how brand identity affects design and content choices has been really valuable for me
For the itterations I've learned to keep improving projects by getting feedback, looking at results, and making things better step by step. This way, I make sure what I deliver is good and stays relevant. It also helps me stay flexible and quick to adapt in any situation.

PROJECT
MEDIA CAMPAIGN

The media campaign project has been a big help in my personal and professional growth. It allowed me to dive deep into creating awareness and developing effective branding strategies. I learned how to craft engaging messages, reach target audience, and use various social media to advertise.

Exploring branding strategy was a valuable experience. I discovered the importance of maintaining a consistent brand identity and strategically positioning it in competitive markets. Working with a team also helped me see things from different perspectives, which greatly stimulated creativity and innovation.

Looking back, this experience has given me valuable skills and confidence in communication. I'm looking forward to apply these insights to future projects and continue exploring new opportunities in this field.

I used HTML and CSS to build the site, and I learned how to use JavaScript to make it interactive.
