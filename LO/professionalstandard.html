PROJECT
MEDIA CAMPAIGN

For this project, we had the same group as in the branding project. Therefore, we kept the contract. This time, we also created a Trello board. We listed all tasks and who was responsible for each one. This allowed us to see who had completed what and what still needed to be done.

We also created a Word document for the media campaign. It outlined what we planned to do, how we would do it, all questions we had, and the answers to them. We also made a presentation for the stakeholder and instructor. We explained why we made certain choices and how we executed them. We received feedback and incorporated it into the next project.

PROJECT-X

In my project to recolor a couch in Stardew Valley, I applied professional standards by organizing tasks effectively and communicating with stakeholders for feedback on colors and design. I conducted research to enhance pixel art techniques that fit the game's style and documented progress and decisions to keep everything transparent and improve the mod over time. This experience has not only sharpened my design skills but also taught me the importance of structured project management and collaborative communication.

CMD METHODS

I used several CMD methods, such as surveys, prototyping, and field research.

Survey: I used surveys to get direct feedback from users. This helped me better understand their needs and preferences, and to better tailor projects to what they really wanted.
Prototyping: Prototyping was necessary as it allowed me to quickly test ideas in models. By doing this, I could quickly see what worked and what didn't before developing further.
Field Trials: After testing my prototypes, I tested my solutions in real situations. This gave me valuable feedback from real users (fellow students and my parents) and helped me adjust my products before continuing development.

This approach allowed me to strengthen my projects with insights and solutions focused on the user. It helped not only in delivering more effective products but also in improving my skills in project management and user experience.

In the future, I want to use better tools for more detailed survey results and more enjoyable prototyping. I also want to improve my skills in analyzing field test data to gain better insights. By applying these methods, I have strengthened my projects with a structured approach focused on user-oriented solutions.
